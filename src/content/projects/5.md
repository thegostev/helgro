## Project Overview

The Sustainable Restaurant Interior embodies eco-conscious dining design that integrates environmental responsibility with culinary excellence. This 12,000 square foot restaurant features interiors made from recycled and sustainable materials, creating a dining experience that aligns with modern values of sustainability and wellness.

## Design Philosophy

Our design philosophy emphasized the use of reclaimed materials, local sourcing, and energy-efficient systems to create a restaurant that not only serves delicious food but also educates and inspires guests about sustainable living. The interiors tell a story of environmental stewardship through thoughtful material choices and functional design.

## Key Features

### Dining Areas

- Communal tables made from reclaimed wood
- Biophilic design elements with living green walls
- Natural lighting maximized through strategic openings
- Acoustic panels made from recycled materials

### Kitchen Integration

- Open kitchen design with sustainable equipment
- Herb gardens integrated into the dining space
- Waste reduction systems visible to guests
- Energy-efficient appliances and lighting

### Outdoor Spaces

- Covered patio with native plant landscaping
- Seasonal outdoor dining areas
- Rainwater harvesting systems
- Composting facilities integrated into the design

## Technical Specifications

- **Total Area**: 12,000 sq ft including outdoor spaces
- **Seating**: 180 indoor and 60 outdoor seats
- **Materials**: 80% recycled and sustainable materials
- **Energy**: Solar panels and energy-efficient systems
- **Water**: Rainwater harvesting and greywater systems
- **Completion**: Q3 2024

## Impact & Recognition

This restaurant has become a model for sustainable hospitality design, earning recognition for its innovative approach to environmental responsibility. The project shows how interior design can contribute to broader sustainability goals while creating enjoyable dining experiences.
