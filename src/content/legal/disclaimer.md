_Last updated: November 12, 2025_

Our services are limited to architectural design, planning, and related consulting. We do not provide:

- Engineering services (structural, mechanical, electrical, plumbing)
- Construction management or supervision

While we maintain professional indemnity insurance, our liability is limited to the extent permitted by applicable law. Clients are advised to obtain their own professional advice and insurance coverage.
